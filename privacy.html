Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 20, 2026

1. Introduction

Tiny Planet ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect and use data when you use our iOS application and website.

2. Data We Collect

  • Location Data: To reveal countries on your map and show your "Puck" position, we request access to your GPS location. This data is processed locally on your device.
  • Visited Countries: We store a list of country identifiers (ISO codes) to sync your map progress.
  • eSIM Transactions: If you purchase an eSIM, we collect minimal data required for order fulfillment (via our partners like Airalo/Stripe). We do not store your credit card details.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following partners to provide our services:

  • Mapbox: For map rendering and geocoding.
  • eSIM Providers: To issue digital SIM cards and data plans.
  • Stripe/Apple Pay: For secure payment processing.

4. Data Retention

Your travel history and visited countries are stored as long as your account is active. You can request data deletion at any time through the app settings or via email.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions, contact us at: support@tinyplanet.app
