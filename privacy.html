Privacy Policy

Updated March 24, 2026

01 / Introduction

Welcome to Tiny Planet. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains our data practices for our mobile application and eSIM ecosystem.

02 / Collection

Account Data: Email and Apple ID via secure Sign in with Apple.

Connectivity: Device identifiers (EID/IMEI) and ICCID required for eSIM activation.

Location: Precise GPS data (with consent) to suggest local plans and build itineraries.

Usage: Anonymized interaction tokens for our Travel AI Guide.

03 / Purpose

Data is used exclusively for service delivery, AI-driven personalization, and providing emergency contact information in our SOS module.

04 / Data Sharing

We share data only with essential partners providing equivalent protection:

Airalo (eSIM Provisioning)
Stripe & Apple Pay
Amplitude (Analytics)
Gemini / OpenAI (AI Logic)

05 / Your Rights

You have the right to access, export, or correct your data at any time.

Account Deletion

You can purge all personal data via Settings > Account > Delete Account. Data is permanently erased from our servers within 30 days.

06 / Compliance

We ensure all global data transfers comply with GDPR (Europe) and CCPA (California) standards.
